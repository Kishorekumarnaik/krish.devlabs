FOUNDER

M. Kishore Kumar Naik

Founder of Krish.DevLabs


M. Kishore Kumar Naik, widely known as Krish, is a self-taught developer and young entrepreneur with a passion for building impactful digital experiences. Currently in his first year of B.Tech in Computer Science at Sri Krishnadevaraya University, he is also pursuing an online B.Sc in Data Science (Foundational Level) from IIT Madras.

Driven by curiosity and a love for innovation, Krish independently learned the tools, technologies, and principles of modern web development. Without formal training, he mastered the skills and executed projects with clarity, precision, and vision far beyond his years.

At just 19, he founded Krish.DevLabs, a digital studio dedicated to creating clean, modern, and user-friendly web solutions that help businesses grow and stand out online. Every product and project reflects his deep understanding of design, technology, and the power of great user experiences.

As the founder, Krish brings originality, sharp problem-solving skills, and a relentless commitment to making a real impact in the digital world.

Empowering your digital presence with modern, scalable web development services.
